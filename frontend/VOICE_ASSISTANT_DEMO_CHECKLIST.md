# Voice Assistant Demo Checklist

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Turn on `Speak responses` (optional).
3. Click the mic button.
4. Expected: assistant enters `Listening for command`.
5. Ask: `What needs my attention today?`
6. Expected: assistant answers and speaks the full response.
7. Wait until speaking ends.
8. Click the mic button again.
9. Ask: `Any fabric shortage?`
10. Expected: assistant answers and speaks the full response.
11. Repeat with:
    - `What is happening with PO-2026-001?`
    - `What can we dispatch today?`
    - `Which mill is late?`
    - `Which contractor should I call first?`
    - `Why was only 11700 dispatched against 12000?`
    - `How many packers are required for PO-2026-004?`
12. Test `Stop speaking` button.
13. Test manual text input and send button.
14. Test browser microphone permission denied behavior.
